John Wick

John Wick had already lost everything that mattered — his beloved wife taken by a terminal illness. But in her final act of love, she left him a small beacon of hope: a puppy, a gift to help him find peace. That peace was shattered when the son of a powerful Russian mob boss broke into his life, stealing his prized 1969 Mustang and mercilessly killing the last gift from his wife. What they didn’t know — what they never could’ve imagined — was that John Wick isn’t just a man in mourning. He’s a legend of the underworld… and they just gave him a reason to come back.

Avatar

On the distant, breathtaking world of Pandora, humanity’s greed threatens to destroy a paradise. Jake Sully, a broken Marine, is thrust into an epic conflict when he’s chosen to inhabit an Avatar—a genetically engineered Na’vi body. As he journeys deep into the heart of Pandora’s wonders and dangers, Jake finds himself torn between duty and a newfound love for the Na’vi people and their way of life. When war erupts, he must choose: stand with those who would conquer, or fight for a world—and a people—worth saving. The fate of Pandora hangs in the balance.
